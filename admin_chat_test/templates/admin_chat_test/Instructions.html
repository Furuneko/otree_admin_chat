GAME RULES

In this study, you will be randomly assigned to a group of four participants.

Each participant in the group begins with 100 points. Each participant must decide how to divide their points between a public and private account. Each point invested in the private account generates 1 ECU for the investor. Each point invested in the public account is multiplied by 2 and then divided evenly among all group members.

If you have any questions about the information above, please contact the experimenter via the chatbox in the bottom right corner of your screen.
